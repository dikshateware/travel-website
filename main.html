Karnataka

The historical places in Karnataka date back to the ancient dynasties all the way to the 6th century. The rock-cut temples of Hampi and Badami, the majestic palaces of Mysore and Bangalore, and the marvellous architecture of the Halebidu temples are all tributes to the magnificence of the kings that have ruled this land, surviving centuries that have witnessed all sorts of change while still retaining their structural integrity.

Jammu and Kashmir is blessed with an incredible topography, marked by fast flowing rivers, high altitude passes, fabulous mountain slopes, lovely forests and mountains that make it possible to enjoy various kinds of adventure activities like trekking, skiing, paragliding, river rafting, mountaineering and angling. Trying these incredible adventure activities in Jammu and Kashmir gives you the ultimate feeling of thrill and adrenaline rush that you are always on the lookout for.
